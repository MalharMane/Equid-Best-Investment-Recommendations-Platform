   Hindustan Unilever Ltd. key Products/Revenue Segments include Personal Care and Other Operating Revenue for the year ending 31-Mar-2022.

For the quarter ended 30-09-2022, the company has reported a Consolidated Total Income of Rs 15,253.00 Crore, up 3.36 % from last quarter Total Income of Rs 14,757.00 Crore and up 16.44 % from last year same quarter Total Income of Rs 13,099.00 Crore. Company has reported net profit after tax of Rs 2,670.00 Crore in latest quarter.

The companys top management includes Mr.Nitin Paranjpe, Mr.Sanjiv Mehta, Mr.Ritesh Tiwari, Mr.Dev Bajpai, Mr.Wilhelmus Uijen, Dr.Ashish Gupta, Ms.Ashu Suyash, Ms.Kalpana Morparia, Mr.Leo Puri, Mr.O P Bhatt, Dr.Sanjiv Misra, Mr.Dev Bajpai. Company has BSR & Co. LLP as its auditors.

As on 30-09-2022, the company has a total of 234.96 Crore shares outstanding.

In 2008, with other member companies of the International Food and Beverage Alliance (IFBA), Unilever committed to the World Health Organization (WHO) that thirdparty auditors would monitor the marketing and advertising principles of the IFBA members. As a result, since 2009, Accenture has been commissioned by IFBA members to independently audit adherence to marketing principles each year.
